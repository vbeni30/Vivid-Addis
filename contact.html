Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my artwork for consideration?
To submit your artwork for consideration, please send an email to submissions@vividaddis.com with your portfolio, artist statement, and contact information. Our curatorial team will review your submission and get back to you within 4-6 weeks.
Do you offer guided tours of the gallery?
Yes, we offer guided tours of our gallery. Tours are available on Wednesdays and Saturdays at 2:00 PM. Please book in advance by calling our office or sending an email to tours@vividaddis.com.
How can I purchase artwork from Vivid Addis?
Artwork can be purchased directly from our gallery or through our online store. For inquiries about specific pieces, please contact our sales team at sales@vividaddis.com or visit us during our business hours.
