Featured Artist

Aida Muluneh

Aida Muluneh is an Ethiopian photographer known for her bold and colorful portraits that challenge traditional narratives about Africa and women. Her work has been exhibited worldwide and is included in the permanent collections of several prestigious museums.

"My goal is to create art that provokes thought and inspires change. Through my lens, I aim to showcase the beauty, complexity, and resilience of Ethiopia and its people."
